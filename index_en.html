LIFE




MOTO

Since I was little, I have always had a passion for engines
passion that I attributed from the whole family, first from my own
father who raised me on motorbikes,
and later by my brother.
I owe everything to this passion, the motorbike for me is a symbol of
freedom, while I go around the curves of the Tuscan hills
I feel free, especially when I go out with my father and mine
brother or with my friends, I love everything about motorcycles, the aesthetics
the performance, the mechanics behind it, I love taking apart and
reassemble my bike over and over again
I love customizing my bike to make it unique
I love speeding around curves and feeling the air inside my helmet
I love the motoring community, we are all brothers with
a single passion, this is bikelife.
